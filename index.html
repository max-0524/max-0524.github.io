Business Challenge

Scholarship information is distributed across multiple departments, causing students to miss opportunities and creating significant administrative workload.

  • Information scattered across Academic Affairs, Student Affairs, International Affairs and Department websites
  • Students frequently miss scholarship deadlines
  • Complex eligibility requirements are difficult to verify manually
  • Administrative staff repeatedly answer the same inquiries
  • No centralized recommendation mechanism exists
